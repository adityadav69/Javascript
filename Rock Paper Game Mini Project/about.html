About Rock-Paper-Scissors Game

The Rock-Paper-Scissors game is a simple yet fun game that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. It is based on the traditional hand game played around the world.

History

Rock-Paper-Scissors is believed to have originated in China during the Han Dynasty. Over time, it spread to Japan and other parts of the world. Today, it is a popular game used to make quick decisions in a fun and engaging way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring this classic game to the digital world, allowing people to play it anytime, anywhere. We aim to provide a simple and enjoyable experience for our users.
